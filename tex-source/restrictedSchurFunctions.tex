The \defin{restricted Schur polynomials} were introduced in 2007
by R. de Mello Koch, J. Smolik, M. Smolik and D. Bekker in 
\cite{KochSmolicSmolic2007i,KochSmolicSmolic2007ii,BekkerKochStephanou2008}.

Their main application seems to be in high-energy physics.


\begin{itemize}

\item 
For definitions and details, see \cite{KochDesseinGiataganasMathwin2011}.

\item

For a thesis account, see \cite{Stephanou2010}.

\item

Restricted Schur polynomials for fermions and integrability in the $su(2|3)$
sector are studied in \cite{KochDiazNokwara2013}.

\end{itemize}
