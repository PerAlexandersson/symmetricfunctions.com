\section[suggestedBooks]{Suggested books}

For an introduction to the topic of symmetric functions and algebraic combinatorics,
I suggest the following books:  
Richard Stanley, \emph{Enumerative Combinatorics II} \cite{StanleyEC2}, 
Lynne Butler, \emph{Subgroup Lattices and Symmetric Functions} \cite{Butler1994}, 
Bruce Sagan, \emph{The Symmetric Group} \cite{Sagan2001}, 
William Fulton, \emph{Young Tableaux} \cite{Fulton1997} and 
Ian Macdonald, \emph{Symmetric functions and Hall polynomials} \cite{Macdonald1995}.

\textbf{Other related books:}
David Bressoud, \emph{Proofs and confirmations: The story of 
the alternating sign matrix conjecture} \cite{Bressoud1999}, which is a very nice reading.
