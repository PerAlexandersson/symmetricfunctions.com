\subsection[klyachko-algebra]{The $q$-Klyachko algebra}

Introduced in \url{https://arxiv.org/pdf/2106.03828}.

It is given by 
\[
   \setQ[\dotsc,u_{-1},u_0,1,u_2,\dotsc] / \langle (q+1)u_i^2 = q u_i u_{i-1} + u_i u_{i+1} \rangle.
\]
